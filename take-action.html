Your Role Matters

You Can Make A Difference

We believe that God wants us to use our gifts and abilities to serve Him. Every person has a role to play in God’s Kingdom — prayerfully consider how you might be part of what God is doing here at Saraland Baptist Church.

Make A Donation

Your financial gifts support the ministry of Saraland Baptist Church — from local outreach and community care to global partnerships like Operation Christmas Child. Every gift, no matter the size, makes a difference for the Kingdom.

We are working to offer secure online giving. In the meantime, please give in person during our services or contact us to discuss other options.

Pray With Us

Prayer Matters

One of the most powerful things you can do is pray. We invite you to pray for our church, our community, and our outreach efforts. If you have a personal prayer request, we would be honored to pray with you and for you.

Join us every Wednesday at 6:00 PM for our corporate Bible Study and Prayer Meeting, where we bring our needs and praises before the Lord together.

Beyond Our Walls

Outreach & Missions

We are committed to sharing the love of Christ both locally and around the world. Here are some of the ways our church reaches beyond our walls.

🎁

Operation Christmas Child

Every year we pack shoeboxes with gifts for children in need through Samaritan’s Purse, sharing the gospel with children around the world.

🌐

Kendrick Ministries

We partner with Kendrick Ministries in their mission work, supporting gospel outreach and community impact locally and internationally.

⛺

Teen Summer Bible Camp

Our teens attend a life-changing summer Bible camp in Colorado, encountering God in creation and returning home with renewed faith and conviction.
