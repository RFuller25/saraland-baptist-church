Our Ministries

A Church That Exalts Jesus and Loves People

We believe that God wants us to use our gifts and abilities to serve Him and love our neighbors. Below are the ministries we offer — there is a place for everyone to grow, serve, and make a difference.

📖

Bible Classes

We have classes from nursery age through adult each Sunday morning. Our teachers are committed to presenting the Scriptures in a relevant and practical manner, helping every age group grow in their understanding of God's Word.

🎤

Choir

Our choir performs a special number on the fourth Sunday of each month and presents seasonal programs throughout the year. The choir practices each Sunday following our Morning Worship Service. All who love to sing for the Lord are welcome to join!

✉

Ms. Ruby's Card Ministry

Meeting on the first Tuesday of each month, this ministry sends birthday cards, thank-you cards, and encouragement cards to members and the community. A simple yet meaningful way to show people they are seen, remembered, and loved.

🧑

Brotherhood Men's Ministry

Men gather every other Saturday morning for breakfast, are challenged by the Word of God, and discuss projects and ways to serve the church and community. A great opportunity for men to sharpen one another in faith and fellowship.

🌿

Youth Ministries

We have classes each Sunday morning to encourage the young people in their walk with the Lord. In addition to regular classes, we offer year-round activities to engage and disciple our youth, including summer Bible camp in Colorado.

🎁

Operation Christmas Child

In partnership with Samaritan's Purse, our congregation fills shoeboxes with gifts and necessities that are sent to children in need around the world, sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ in a tangible way.

🌐

Kendrick Ministries Partnership

We partner with Kendrick Ministries in their mission work, supporting gospel outreach and community impact both locally and internationally. Together, we seek to carry the love of Christ to those who need it most.

⛺

Summer Bible Camp

Each year our teens attend a summer Bible camp in Colorado, a transformative experience in God's creation that helps young people deepen their faith, build lasting friendships, and grow in their relationship with the Lord.
