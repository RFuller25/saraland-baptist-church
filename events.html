What's Happening

Events

Stay Connected with Our Church Family

Every Week

Regular Gatherings

Beyond special events, we gather faithfully every week for worship, study, and fellowship. All are welcome!

📖

Sunday Bible Classes

Every Sunday — 9:00 AM

Classes for all ages from nursery through adult. A great way to dig deeper into God's Word in community.

✍

Morning Worship Service

Every Sunday — 10:00 AM

Our main Sunday service featuring worship through music, prayer, and preaching from God's Word.

🕐

Wednesday Bible Study & Prayer

Every Wednesday — 6:00 PM

Midweek gathering for in-depth Bible study and a time of corporate prayer together as a church family.

☼

Thursday Morning Bible Study

Every Thursday — 10:00 AM

A morning Bible study opportunity for those available during the week to gather and study Scripture.

Monthly & Seasonal

Ministry Events

🎤

Choir Sunday

4th Sunday of Each Month — The choir presents a special number during the morning worship service, with seasonal presentations throughout the year.

✉

Card Ministry Meeting

1st Tuesday of Each Month — Ms. Ruby's Card Ministry gathers to write and send birthday, thank-you, and encouragement cards to members and the community.

🧑

Brotherhood Men's Breakfast

Bimonthly Saturday Mornings — Men gather for breakfast, a challenge from God's Word, and fellowship as they discuss how to serve the church and community.
