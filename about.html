Who We Are

Friendly · Loving · Grounded in God's Word

Our Mission

We Desire to Be a Church That Exalts Jesus and Loves People

We are a friendly, loving congregation whose greatest desire is to help every person strengthen their relationship with Jesus Christ. We believe that God's Word is fundamental to spiritual growth, and we strive to make Scripture accessible, relevant, and life-changing for everyone who walks through our doors.

Whether you are a new believer, a long-time follower of Christ, or simply someone who is curious about faith — you are welcome here. We desire for people to have a vibrant relationship with God, and strive to assist them in the process of this becoming a reality.

We welcome people from all backgrounds, and we invite you to come as you are and discover the life-changing love of Jesus Christ together with us.
